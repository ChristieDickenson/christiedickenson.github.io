Perfect Buttercream Icing

Ingredients

  • CUP Unsalted Butter
  • 1 CUP High Ratio Shortening
  • LBS Powdered Sugar
  • 2 TBS Clear Vanilla Extract
  • Water
  • Gel Food coloring (Optional)


Directions

  1. Place butter, shortening, and vanilla extract in a mixing bowl and mix together.

  2. Slowly add powdered sugar 1 cup at a time and mix well.

  3. Add water 1 TBS at a time as needed to thin the icing to the desired consistency

  4. Add food coloring a little at a time and mix well until you have achieved the desired color


You are now ready to ice your cake with your fresh delicious buttercream icing!


Note: Icing may be stored in the refridgerator for up to one week or freezer for up to 3 months.


Substitutions: You may also omit the unsalted butter and use another cup of shortening instead and add 1 TBS of clear butter flavoring
